Cayman Islands


Overview

The Cayman Islands are a British overseas territory, compromising of three islands: Grand Cayman, Cayman Brac and Little Cayman. These Islands are located to the south of Cuba and the northeast of Honduras.

Island Facts

  • Size: 76 sq miles (Grand Cayman), 14 sq miles (Cayman Brac), 10 sq miles (Little Cayman)
  • Average temperature: 27 degrees
  • Population: 69,475 (across all three Islands)
  • Sea: Caribbean
  • Official Language: English

Things to do

  • Stingray City
  • Rum Point Beach
  • Seven Mile Beach
  • Sunset boat trip
